About Me

I am a veteran of the United States Air Force. I spent the last 6 years as an Air Traffic Controller and Landing Zone Controller. Over the years I've got to experience alot of cool and amazing experiences (like shutting down Bagram Air Base the largest US Military Base in Afghanistan this past year), but the military was always just a passing point and not my final destination in my life journey.

Now I turn my attention back to what I aspired to be apart of as a young kid, the Tech Industry. I had gone to College for Computer Programming and it tought me the basics and fueled me to self-learn more. As I grew in my knowledge of software development I began to gravitate towards Web Development and Mobile Application Development. I look forward to what life has to bring me now and I am ready for whatever challenge is brought my way.
